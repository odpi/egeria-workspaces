01

Governance classifications & security have no browsing surface

Highest value

Confidentiality, retention, criticality, and security-tag classifications drive most real governance workflows, but there's no tab to browse zones, subject areas, or "show me everything tagged Confidential." The data exists — it just surfaces incidentally, buried in raw element headers a couple of handlers happen to read.

ConfidentialityConfidence CriticalityRetention ImpactSecurityTags OwnershipZoneMembership GovernanceZoneSubjectArea SecurityGroupSecurityRole SecurityAccessControl
Current state —zoneMembership read incidentally in glossary_handler.py / tech_catalog_handler.py; no dedicated tab anywhere.

02

Glossary terms don't show their semantic relationships

Fixed — 2026-07-15

This gap was smaller than it looked: classifications and term-to-term relationships were already being fetched and rendered. The real bug — confirmed live, not just by reading the roadmap doc — was that Egeria puts every term-to-term relationship type into one undifferentiated relatedTerms list rather than separate per-type keys, so a Synonym link and an ISARelationship link were indistinguishable in the UI. Fixed by grouping each entry by its actual relationship type (and, for the asymmetric ISARelationship, which end the term is on).

SynonymAntonym ISARelationshipPreferredTerm RelatedTermUsedInContext AbstractConcept
Fix —glossary_handler.py's _group_related_terms(); verified end-to-end with seeded test data via egeria-python/tests/functional-tests/test_glossary_manager_omvs.py::test_term_relationship_keys_synonym_and_isa. Still open, separately: term → semantically-linked-asset back-links (a different relationship, see Extending the TypeExplorer.md → Cross-section navigation).
