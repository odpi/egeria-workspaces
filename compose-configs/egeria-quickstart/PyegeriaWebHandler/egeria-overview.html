Prototype mockup · sample Coco Pharmaceuticals figures. Every tile maps to data the portal already exposes — asset/type counts from the Tech Catalog handlers, classification & zone tallies from /api/insights/summary, certifications from the Audit handler, glossary counts from the Glossary handler, survey coverage from survey-report annotations, the change stream from Context Events, and the People & Community block from the Community Profile / Collaboration handlers (actor, community, karma / egeria-feedback likes·ratings·comments, informal-tags). The Perspective → Question framing is the existing perspectives_handler model (Perspective ⟶ ScopedBy ⟶ Question), with each question answered by a saved report spec / view definition. Trend lines use Egeria's native asOfTime historical queries — no separate time-series store required. The one new backend piece is a small /api/overview/* aggregation router that fans these out and caches them (mirroring the operations handler's non-blocking cache pattern).
