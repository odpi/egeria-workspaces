# My Egeria

My Egeria is a terminal-style (Textual) view of your own presence in Egeria — profile, roles, teams, and actions — running as a browser-hosted TUI rather than a web page.

Access it from the portal tile or directly at `/my-egeria/`.

---

## What it shows

A profile-centred view of the currently connected Egeria user: personal details, assigned roles, team memberships, and to-do actions, plus a data-catalog browsing view.

---

## Early-stage tool

My Egeria is newer and less polished than the other portal tools — it's a terminal UI streamed into the browser (via `textual-serve`) rather than a native web app, so interactions (scrolling, clicking) feel a little different from the rest of the portal. Some personas may occasionally hit a permissions error loading the profile; if the screen doesn't render, try reloading, and if that doesn't help, it's a known rough edge rather than something you're doing wrong.
